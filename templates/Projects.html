IKUNDE INITIATIVE

"Ikunde" is a kinyarwanda word that means "love yourself."

“Ikunde” is an initiative under LIHOZA VOICE with a vision and mission to advocate for self-value and to stimulate its traits within the youth and women with a focus on mental wellbeing.

IKUNDE | Pillars

Self-Value Mental health Community Building

IKUNDE | Social Issues

“Ikunde” aims to address social issues that arise as a result of failure to positively reinforce the aforementioned pillars.

Mental Health Disorders Drugs & Substance Abuse Gender Based Violence

Economic Deprivation

Childhood Emotional Neglect
